Music

I find music to be one the things that can most quickly affect my mood. Here are a few different types of music that I like to listen to depending on what I need in that moment. I keep playlists of these types of music on Youtube and one my phone for easy access when I want to listen.

Thankful

I find this one to be preventative. Listening to people sing about how thankful they are for what they have inspires me. I say pair with dancing like a fool for maximum impact

I find Soca to be a genre that has so much of this. I'm loving this song and this one and this one right now

Sassy

When I really just need to say "No way. I'm rising above X", it can help to channel a bit of rebellion

Calming

This is especially helpful if I'm feeling anxious.

Silly

Sometimes you just need a laugh. For a funny story told in song, calypso's got some good ones.
